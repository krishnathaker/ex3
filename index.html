Van Gogh's The Starry Night

My most favorite piece of art is The Starry Night. The Starry Night is one of Vincent Van Gogh's finest oil canvas paintings.

Van Gogh was a popular post-impressionist artist. He created this painting in June of 1889. The painting depicts the view from the east-facing window of Van Gogh's asylum room at Saint-Remy-de-Provence, just before sunrise, with the addition of an idealist village.

This painting has been a part of the permanent collection at the Museaum of Modern Art (MoMA) in New York City, since 1941.

I enjoy this painting because I find the different shades of blue very soothing. I also like the small-stroke method of painting that Van Gogh used to depict the stars and moon.
